The "Brain-in-a-vat" objection to [[Behaviorism]] (also known as the *paralytic objection*) puts forward the following scenario:

Imagine your brain is removed from your body, kept alive in nutrient fluid and connected to a supercomputer that feeds it exactly the same neural inputs it would normally receive. (or if your motor functions are completely paralysed but your sensory functions and mental faculties remain completely intact) Internally you still have pains, desires, visual experiences, thoughts, etc., yet your severed brain has no possibility of producing the bodily behaviour that behaviourists take to constitute those states.
