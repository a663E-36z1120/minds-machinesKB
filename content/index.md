This is an [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)/[Quartz](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz) knowledge base for ideas and information related to the philosphy of minds and machines, starting with the contents of the course [PHL342H1: Minds and Machines](https://artsci.calendar.utoronto.ca/course/phl342h1) at the University of Toronto taught by [Prof. Sara Aronowitz](https://philosophy.utoronto.ca/directory/sara-aronowitz/) (Winter 2025). I do intend to gradually grow this out further with my past and future readings in this area.

Tag index + definitions:
- #mind: a mind, biological or artificial.
- #idea: an idea that a mind came up with.
- #person: a human being.
- #machine: a machine, physical or abstract.
- #algorithm: a procedure that a machine runs (or mathematical formalism for a problem).
- #problem: a problem or a question.
- #property: a property that a mind, machine, idea, or algorithm can possess.
