Two ways to understand moral judgement:  
1. An **observer-perspective verdict** on whether an actor’s action is morally bad, good, or neutral. 
2. An **actor-perspective decision** on what they should do, morally speaking (moral motivation).

Debate surrounds if these two judgements are the products of the same cognitive system.

**No:** [[Kevin Kelly]]
